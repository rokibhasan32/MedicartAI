Frequently Asked Questions

Can pharmacists prescribe medications?

While pharmacists cannot prescribe most medications, they can provide expert advice on proper usage, side effects, and recommend over-the-counter alternatives when appropriate.

How quickly will I get a response?

Most questions are answered within 4-6 hours during business hours. Urgent matters receive priority and are typically addressed within 1-2 hours.

Is this service confidential?

Yes, all consultations are completely confidential. Your personal health information is protected and only accessible to our licensed pharmacy team.

What information should I provide?

Please include medication names, dosages, your symptoms, and any other relevant health information to help us provide the best advice.
