Ordering Questions

How do I place an order?

You can place an order through our website by browsing products, adding them to your cart, and proceeding to checkout. You can also upload prescriptions or request specific medications through our medicine request service.

Do I need a prescription to order medicines?

Prescription requirements depend on the medication. Over-the-counter medications can be ordered without a prescription. For prescription medications, you'll need to upload a valid prescription from a licensed healthcare provider.

Can I modify or cancel my order?

You can modify or cancel your order within 1 hour of placing it, provided it hasn't been processed for shipping. Contact our customer service team immediately for assistance with order modifications or cancellations.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, mobile banking, and cash on delivery (where available). All online payments are secured with SSL encryption for your safety.

Delivery Questions

What are your delivery areas and times?

We deliver to most urban areas. Standard delivery takes 2-3 business days. Express delivery (1-2 days) and same-day delivery are available in select areas. Delivery times are typically between 9 AM and 8 PM.

Do you offer emergency delivery?

Yes, we offer emergency delivery services for critical medications. Our emergency service operates 24/7 with delivery times ranging from 30 minutes to 4 hours depending on the urgency and location.

How much does delivery cost?

Standard delivery is free for orders above $50. For orders below $50, a small delivery fee applies. Express and emergency delivery services have additional charges based on location and urgency.

Can I track my order?

Yes, you can track your order through your account dashboard. We provide real-time tracking updates and SMS/email notifications about your order status and estimated delivery time.

Prescription Questions

How do I upload a prescription?

You can upload your prescription through our website or mobile app. Simply go to the prescription upload section, take a clear photo of your prescription, and submit it. Our pharmacists will verify it within 1-2 hours.

What information should be on the prescription?

Your prescription should include: patient's full name, medication name, dosage instructions, quantity, prescribing doctor's information, and date of prescription. Make sure all text is clearly visible in the photo.

How long does prescription verification take?

Prescription verification typically takes 1-2 hours during business hours. For prescriptions submitted outside business hours, verification will be completed the next business day. You'll receive notification once verified.

Can I get refills for my prescription?

Yes, we can process prescription refills if authorized by your doctor. You can request refills through your account, and we'll coordinate with your healthcare provider if additional authorization is needed.

General Questions

Are your medicines genuine and safe?

Yes, all our medicines are sourced directly from licensed manufacturers and distributors. We maintain strict quality control measures and only supply genuine, FDA-approved medications.

What is your return policy?

Due to safety regulations, we cannot accept returns of prescription medications. Unopened over-the-counter products can be returned within 7 days of delivery. Contact customer service for assistance with returns.

How can I contact customer service?

You can contact our customer service team through phone, email, or live chat. Our support hours are 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM daily. For emergency assistance, our 24/7 emergency line is always available.

Do you offer pharmacist consultations?

Yes, we offer free pharmacist consultations through our website, mobile app, and phone. Our licensed pharmacists can provide medication advice, answer questions about side effects, and help with dosage instructions.
